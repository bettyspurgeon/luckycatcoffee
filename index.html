our story

In 2012, Jane was vacationing in her ancestral country of Barbados. She was driving to St. Lawrence Gap one afternoon when she saw a small kitten in the street. She picked him up and took him to the vet. He had so much wrong with him. His recovery was long and difficult, but he made it. Jane decided to name him Lucky and brought him back to Canada.


During Lucky's recovery, he had to be swaddled and held upright for 30 minutes after every meal. Every day after feeding him, Jane took him around the house in his "purrito" and had him smell various things. He always loved smelling the coffee and he always tried to take a bite of every doughnut or pastry.


In 2015 Jane and Lucky travelled through Europe and Asia. Jane and Lucky smelled (and tasted) so many different coffees and loved them all. Jane and Lucky moved to Luxembourg shortly after their trip as they met Lucky's dad here, Dave.


In 2021, Jane and Lucky founded Lucky Cat coffee company. Every day Jane and Lucky come to work and make beautiful coffee creations and delightful doughnuts inspired by their travels. Lucky has paw-picked every coffee they sell in the truck and they can't wait to share them with you.
